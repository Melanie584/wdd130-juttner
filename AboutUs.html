About Us

Sidus is a Whitelist Minecraft server, currently oriented towards survival gameplay. While we have plans to expand, we are in the planning phase and are excited to invite new, responsible, and sensible players to apply.

If you are applying to be whitelisted, please include your Minecraft username and a brief paragraph explaining your playstyle and intentions. If you have a reference from a current whitelist player, you are more likely to be accepted.

We are actively looking for more trustworthy staff to help run and expand the server. If you are interested in building, moderating, or helping in any way, contact a current staff member. Staff are expected to help resolve player conflicts while online. Extended inactivity may result in loss of staff position.

Follow and join us on other platforms!
